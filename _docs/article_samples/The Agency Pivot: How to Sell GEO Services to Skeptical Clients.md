## Detailed Audit Workflow: Step-by-Step

**To close Package 1 & 2, you need a process.** Here's how to run an audit:

### Phase 1: Keyword Research (2 hours)

**Step 1:** Identify 30 target keywords

* Mix of informational + comparative intent
* Keywords where you rank #1-5 on Google
* Keywords where customers search (ask your sales team)

**Example for SaaS CRM:**

* "Best CRM for small businesses"
* "HubSpot vs. Salesforce"
* "How to choose a CRM"
* "Most affordable CRM software"
* "CRM for nonprofits"
* "Best CRM for B2B SaaS"
* ... (repeat 24 more)

**Step 2:** Create tracking spreadsheet

```
Keyword | ChatGPT | Perplexity | Google AI | Claude | Sentiment | Notes

Best CRM | Yes #2 | Yes #1 | Yes #3 | No | Positive | Leader quote

HubSpot vs SF | Yes #1 | Yes #1 | Yes #1 | Yes #2 | Neutral | Balanced comparison
```


---

### Phase 2: The Manual Spot-Check (3-4 hours)

**Step 1:** Open ChatGPT (with search enabled)

**Step 2:** Paste keyword #1

**Step 3:** Read the response. Document:

* Does your brand appear? (Yes/No)
* Where? (First sentence, middle, end, footnote)
* Sentiment? (Positive "best for...", Neutral "also offers", Negative "lacks...", Not mentioned)
* Which competitor was cited? (Note their brand)

**Step 4:** Repeat for Perplexity, Google AI, Claude

**Step 5:** Repeat for all 30 keywords

**Estimated time:** 4-5 hours total (you can batch this with a junior team member)


---

### Phase 3: Data Analysis & Report (2 hours)

**Step 1:** Calculate Share of Voice

```
Formula: (Your mentions ÷ Total mentions) × 100

Example:
Your mentions: 45

Competitor A: 62

Competitor B: 41

Competitor C: 28

Total: 176

Your SOV = (45 ÷ 176) × 100 = 25.6%
```

**Step 2:** Calculate sentiment score

```
Positive mentions: 30

Neutral mentions: 12

Negative mentions: 3

Total: 45

Sentiment score = (30 ÷ 45) × 100 = 66.7% positive
```

**Step 3:** Identify gaps

* "We rank #1 on Google but #3 on ChatGPT"
* "We're not mentioned in Google AI Overviews for 40% of queries"
* "Our sentiment is 66% positive, competitor is 82%"

**Step 4:** Create slides (10-page report)

* Executive summary (1 page)
* Visibility breakdown by platform (2 pages)
* Competitive analysis (2 pages)
* Sentiment analysis (1 page)
* Gaps & opportunities (2 pages)
* Recommendations (2 pages)
